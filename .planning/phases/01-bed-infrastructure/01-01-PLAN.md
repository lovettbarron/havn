**Line items to include (both options):**

Beds:
- 3x backyard beds: byJEMA CUBY Corten B60xH40, L120cm at 1,445 DKK each (Option A) / byJEMA CUBY Galvanized B60xH40, L120cm (Option B -- price from research ~1,245 DKK each)
- 2x terrace beds: byJEMA CUBY Galvanized, custom 80x40x30cm or fallback 80x40x40cm at ~1,245 DKK each (both options)
- Note: Add a footnote that terrace bed dimensions (30cm height) need confirmation from byJEMA -- 40cm height is the fallback

Soil and fill:
- Hojbedsmuld (raised bed soil): ~0.6 m3 total for top layers of all 5 beds. Supplier: sandshoppen.dk or droemmehave.dk. Price: ~1,500 DKK/m3, so ~900-1,200 DKK. Add note to get delivery quote for Vejle.
- Drainage gravel (singels 16-32mm): ~0.15 m3. Source: local byggemarked (Silvan, Bauhaus). ~300 DKK.
- Garden waste fill: 0 DKK (sourced from own garden -- branches, leaves, grass clippings)

Slug defense:
- Copper tape 50mm self-adhesive: 2 rolls of 25m. Source: Amazon.de or byggemarked. ~150-250 DKK per roll.

Reservoir materials (for 2 beds -- tomato and cucumber):
- PVC pipe 50mm: 6 meters + 4x 90-degree elbows + 4x end caps. Byggemarked. ~200-400 DKK total.
- Pond liner / EPDM 0.5mm+: 2 pieces, each sized to line one bed interior with 15cm extra up the sides. Byggemarked. ~300-500 DKK.
- Geotextile fabric: 2 pieces to cover gravel reservoir layer in each bed. Byggemarked. ~100-200 DKK.
- Overflow fittings: 2x bulkhead fittings or drilled pipe stubs. Byggemarked. ~50-100 DKK.

Trellis materials (for cucumber bed):
- Frame: 4x pressure-treated 45x45mm timber battens, 150cm length, OR 4x galvanized steel angle 25x25mm, 150cm. Byggemarked. ~300-500 DKK.
- Climbing surface: galvanized wire mesh (10x10cm grid), 120x120cm panel, OR UV-resistant nylon netting. Byggemarked. ~100-200 DKK.
- Hardware: galvanized bolts, hinges, or brackets for ridge connection. ~50-100 DKK.

Tools/consumables (if not already owned):
- Rubbing alcohol (for cleaning steel before copper tape). ~30 DKK.
- Drill + 10-12mm bit (for PVC pipe holes). Note: "skip if you already have a drill."

**Totals:**
- Option A total: Sum all line items. Should land around 8,500-10,200 DKK per research estimates.
- Option B total: Sum all line items. Should land around 7,900-10,200 DKK.
- Add a "Potential Savings" section noting: garden waste is free, gravel can be sourced cheaply from local suppliers, and the biggest cost driver is the beds themselves.

**Ordering tips section:**
- byJEMA: 1-3 day handling + 2-7 day transit. Order early.
- Soil: Get delivery quote for your address in Vejle before ordering. Compare sandshoppen.dk and droemmehave.dk.
- Terrace beds: Contact byJEMA to confirm 80x40x30cm availability. If not available, 80x40x40cm works (10cm taller, ~20 kg heavier when saturated).
- Copper tape: Apply on assembly day before corten patina develops (better adhesion on new steel).

Use RESEARCH.md pricing data. Where exact prices are uncertain, show a range and note "confirm with supplier."

- Include an ASCII diagram showing:

  - Bed positions along railing
  - Dimensions (80x40x30cm each)

**Weight assessment for terrace beds:**
- Calculate weight per bed: 80x40x30cm = 0.096 m3 volume
- Saturated soil density: ~1,700 kg/m3
- Maximum saturated weight: ~163 kg per bed
- Realistic with drainage layer and lighter fill: 100-130 kg per bed
- Bed weight (empty galvanized steel): ~15-20 kg estimate
- Total per bed when full and saturated: 115-150 kg
- Note: Previous owners had two small beds plus furniture -- provides precedent
- Recommendation: Position over structural supports (carport walls/beams), not over unsupported spans
- Include a clear "Weight Summary" table

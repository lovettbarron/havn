  <action>
Create `docs/setup-guide.md` -- the master construction sequence that tells the user exactly what to do and in what order, from the day materials arrive through the first watering.

**Document structure:**

1. **Overview** -- This guide sequences the complete garden infrastructure build. Estimated total time: 6-8 hours across 1-2 weekends. All materials should be ordered using `shopping-list.md` and delivered before starting. Bed positions should be marked out using `bed-layout.md` on the day before build day.

2. **Before You Start checklist:**
   - [ ] All materials from `shopping-list.md` have arrived
   - [ ] Bed positions marked on the ground per `bed-layout.md`
   - [ ] Garden waste collected (branches, leaves, clippings) for middle layer
   - [ ] Water tap confirmed working and hose attached
   - [ ] Drill with 10-12mm bit available (for reservoir PVC)
   - [ ] Clear weather forecast for build day(s)

3. **Day 1: Bed Assembly + Reservoirs + Slug Defense (~4 hours)**

   Step 1: Assemble backyard beds (Beds 1-3) (~45 min)
   - Time: ~15 min per bed
   - People: 1-2 adults
   - Child can: Hold parts during assembly, hand over screws/bolts
   - Assemble byJEMA CUBY beds per manufacturer instructions at the positions marked from `bed-layout.md`
   - Verify: All 3 beds are level, positioned per layout, all sides accessible with 70cm paths

   Step 2: Install reservoirs in Beds 1 and 2 (~60 min)
   - Time: ~30 min per bed
   - People: 1 adult (child should step back -- sharp tools and heavy liner)
   - Follow `reservoir-build.md` completely for each bed
   - CRITICAL: Test each reservoir with water before proceeding. Fill via tube until overflow triggers. Fix any issues now -- they are impossible to fix after soil is added.
   - Verify: Water fills via tube, exits via overflow, no leaks visible

   Step 3: Apply copper tape to all backyard beds (~30 min)
   - Time: ~10 min per bed
   - People: 1 adult
   - Child can: Hold the tape roll, press tape firmly against the bed
   - Follow `slug-defense.md` for application method
   - Apply IMMEDIATELY on new steel (before patina develops on corten beds)
   - Verify: Continuous band around each bed, no gaps at corners, overlapped at joints

   Step 4: Assemble terrace beds (Beds 4-5) (~30 min)
   - Time: ~15 min per bed
   - People: 1-2 adults (carrying beds upstairs)
   - Child can: Supervise placement from safe distance
   - Assemble and position per `bed-layout.md` terrace section
   - Position over structural supports (above carport walls/beams)
   - Verify: Beds along railing, over structural supports, level

   Step 5: Apply copper tape to terrace beds (~20 min)
   - Follow `slug-defense.md`
   - Verify: Same standard as backyard beds

4. **Day 2: Soil Fill + Trellis (~3-4 hours)**

   Step 6: Fill Beds 1 and 2 (reservoir beds) (~45 min)
   - Time: ~20 min per bed
   - People: 1-2 adults
   - Child can: Spread the top hojbedsmuld layer (fun, dirty, and manageable)
   - Reservoir is already installed (Day 1). Add garden waste layer on top of geotextile, then hojbedsmuld. See `soil-layers.md` noting the reservoir modification for these beds.
   - Verify: Layers at correct depths, fill tube accessible above soil line

   Step 7: Fill Bed 3 (standard bed) (~20 min)
   - Follow `soil-layers.md` for standard 40cm bed: drainage gravel, garden waste, hojbedsmuld
   - Child can: Help spread all three layers
   - Verify: Drainage layer 8-10cm, waste layer 10-15cm, soil layer 15-20cm

   Step 8: Fill Beds 4 and 5 (terrace beds) (~30 min)
   - Follow `soil-layers.md` for 30cm terrace bed variant with lightweight materials (LECA/perlite, coir)
   - Transport soil materials upstairs in buckets
   - Child can: Help carry small buckets of soil
   - Verify: Total fill height ~25-28cm (allowing for settling)

   Step 9: Build and install A-frame trellis on Bed 2 (~60 min)
   - Time: ~60 min
   - People: 1-2 adults
   - Child can: Hold mesh panels during attachment, test stability after
   - Follow `trellis-build.md` completely
   - Position over Bed 2 (cucumber bed) with legs on either side
   - Anchor to bed or ground
   - Verify: Stable under firm push, mesh securely attached, peak at ~120cm

   Step 10: First watering (~15 min)
   - Water all beds thoroughly from the top
   - Fill both reservoirs via fill tubes until overflow triggers
   - Let water soak in, water again
   - Beds will settle 5-10cm over the next week -- plan to top up with hojbedsmuld before planting (Phase 2)

5. **Completion Checklist:**
   - [ ] 3 backyard beds assembled and positioned per layout
   - [ ] 2 terrace beds assembled and positioned over structural supports
   - [ ] Reservoirs installed and tested in Beds 1 and 2
   - [ ] Copper tape applied to all 5 beds (no gaps)
   - [ ] All beds filled with correct soil layers
   - [ ] A-frame trellis installed on Bed 2, stable and anchored
   - [ ] Fill tubes accessible above soil in Beds 1 and 2
   - [ ] All beds watered thoroughly
   - [ ] Ready for Phase 2: planting

6. **Document Index** -- list all Phase 1 docs with one-line description:
   - `shopping-list.md` -- What to buy (dual-priced)
   - `bed-layout.md` -- Where to put it (with diagrams)
   - `soil-layers.md` -- How to fill beds
   - `slug-defense.md` -- Copper tape application
   - `reservoir-build.md` -- Self-watering system build
   - `trellis-build.md` -- Cucumber A-frame build
   - `setup-guide.md` -- This document (master sequence)

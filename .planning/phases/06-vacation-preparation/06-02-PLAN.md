**File 1: `docs/vacation-countdown-script.md`** -- Full session script for the 3-day pre-departure countdown.

Follow the established session script format from `docs/warm-season-session-scripts.md` and `docs/planting-day-script.md`. Each day is a mini-session with the same structure:
- Pre-session prep checklist (father, before child joins)
- Motor skill split table (who does what)
- Timed steps with minute estimates
- Transition cues between activities
- Mandatory breaks after high-effort segments
- Clear "done" signals

**Day -3: Mulching Day ("Tucking the Plants In")**
- Duration target: 45-60 min including break
- Vibe: Sensory, nurturing
- Prep: Father positions straw near backyard beds, bark near terrace beds, depth-check stick (marked 5cm/8cm), snack ready
- Step 1: Take bed photos for neighbor guide (5 min) -- beds look their best freshly planted
- Step 2: Backyard beds straw mulch together (20 min)
- Break: Snack (5-10 min)
- Step 3: Terrace beds bark mulch together (10 min)
- Step 4: Deep water ALL beds -- child does watering (15 min)
- Say: "We're tucking the plants in for their nap while we're away"
- Done signal: All beds have 5-8cm mulch confirmed with depth stick

**Day -2: Reservoir Fill and Big Harvest Party**
- Duration target: 40-50 min including break
- Vibe: Celebration, abundance
- Prep: Father fills all reservoirs via fill tubes (10 min, before child joins). Harvest basket/bowl ready. Camera for harvest photos.
- Step 1: Harvest walk -- child leads, starting with "his beds" first (highest engagement). Pick everything showing color: tomatoes, cucumbers, berries, beans, herbs. (20 min, 30 min max cap)
- Break/transition: Bring harvest inside, wash and display (10 min)
- Step 2: Count and celebrate -- how many tomatoes? biggest cucumber? (5 min)
- If child loses interest after 20 min: father finishes remaining harvest while child does different activity
- Done signal: All ripe produce picked, reservoirs full

**Day -1: Neighbor Walk-Through and Goodbye**
- Duration target: 30-40 min (neighbor portion) + 10 min (goodbye)
- Vibe: Handoff, closure, reassurance
- Prep: Father prints neighbor guide (insert Day -3 photos if possible). Two copies (primary + backup neighbor).
- Step 1: Neighbor walk-through (15-20 min per locked decision):
  - Route: Start at water tap location, then Bed A -> B -> C (backyard), then terrace D -> E
  - At each bed: show what is growing, what might be ripe, how to water
  - Show reservoir fill tubes on reservoir beds
  - Hand over printed guide as backup reference
  - Emphasize: "text with a photo if anything looks wrong, most things can wait"
  - Ripe produce policy: neighbor keeps whatever they harvest
- Step 2: Deliver backup copy to second neighbor (5 min)
- Step 3: Final top-up watering -- child does all beds (10 min)
- Step 4: Child says goodbye to each bed (5 min) -- emotional closure, reduces anxiety about leaving
- Done signal: Neighbor has guide, all beds watered, child has said goodbye

**General notes across all 3 days:**
- Weather abort: If heavy rain on any day, shift that day's activities by one day (adjust countdown)
- Meltdown protocol: If child becomes distressed, stop current activity, complete remaining tasks without child. No pushing through.
- Each day has a clear start-to-finish arc that feels complete

**File 2: `docs/pre-departure-checklist.md`** -- One-page printable checklist.

Design for printing and taping to fridge. Format:
- Title: "Vacation Prep Checklist" (large, clear)
- Grouped by day with checkboxes
- Each item is a concrete action (not a category)
- Use specific bed references and actions, not vague descriptions

```
## Day -3: Mulching Day
- [ ] Take photos of each bed for neighbor guide
- [ ] Spread straw mulch on Beds A, B, C (5-8cm depth)
- [ ] Spread bark mulch on Beds D, E (5-8cm depth)
- [ ] Deep water all 5 beds after mulching

## Day -2: Harvest Party
- [ ] Fill all reservoirs via fill tubes
- [ ] Harvest everything showing color (tomatoes, cucumbers, berries, beans)
- [ ] Wash and count the harvest together

## Day -1: Neighbor Day
- [ ] Print 2 copies of neighbor guide (insert photos)
- [ ] Walk primary neighbor through garden (15-20 min)
- [ ] Hand printed guide to primary neighbor
- [ ] Deliver backup copy to second neighbor
- [ ] Final top-up watering all beds
- [ ] Child says goodbye to each bed
```

Keep to one page. No explanations -- just action items with checkboxes.

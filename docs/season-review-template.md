## How to Use This Template

Walk both beds together with your child before autumn cleanup. This is a 5-minute sensory activity, not a quiz.

1. Walk to Bed A. Point at each section (south, middle, north). Ask the child: "Which one was your favorite?" and "Which one didn't you like?"
2. Walk to Bed B. Same questions.
3. Father records the child's answers -- the child does not need to fill anything in.
4. After the walk, father completes the scorecard below using observations from the whole season.

The most valuable data point is what the child cared about. Everything else is secondary.

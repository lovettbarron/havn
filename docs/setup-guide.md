# Master Setup Guide: Build Day Sequence

**Project:** Havn -- A Learning Garden
**Date:** 2026-03-28

This guide sequences the complete garden infrastructure build from the day materials arrive through the first watering. Follow the steps in order -- each step builds on the previous one, and some things (like reservoirs and copper tape) must happen before others (like adding soil).

**Estimated total time:** 6-7.5 hours across 2 days.

**Before you start:** All materials should be ordered using [shopping-list.md](shopping-list.md) and delivered before starting. Bed positions should be marked out using [bed-layout.md](bed-layout.md) on the day before build day.

---

## Before You Start Checklist

- [ ] All materials from [shopping-list.md](shopping-list.md) have arrived
- [ ] Bed positions marked on the ground per [bed-layout.md](bed-layout.md)
- [ ] Garden waste collected (branches, leaves, clippings) for middle layer
- [ ] Water tap confirmed working and hose attached
- [ ] Drill with 10-12mm bit available (for reservoir PVC)
- [ ] Clear weather forecast for build day(s)

## Day 1: Mole Mesh + Bed Assembly + Reservoir + Slug Defense (~3.5-4.5 hours)

### Step 1: Lay Mole Mesh at Bed A and Bed B Positions (~15 min)

- **Time:** ~7 min per bed
- **People:** 1 adult
- **Child can:** Help hold mesh in place

Lay a sheet of 6mm galvanized hardware cloth (volieretrad) at each of the 2 marked grass positions. Cut each sheet ~10cm wider than the bed footprint on all sides. The excess will fold up against the inner walls after the bed is placed on top.

- **Bed A:** 140x80cm sheet (for 120x60 bed)
- **Bed B:** 250x100cm sheet (for 230x80 bed)

This prevents moles and voles from tunneling up into the growing medium from below. Moles are common in Vejle -- the mesh is cheap insurance.

**Note:** Skip this for Bed C -- it sits on cobblestones, not grass.

**Verify:** Mesh flat at each position, no gaps at corners, sized to fold up inside the bed walls.

### Step 2: Assemble Bed A (120x60) and Bed B (230x80) on Grass (~30 min)

- **Time:** ~15 min per bed
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Hold parts during assembly, hand over screws/bolts

Assemble the two byJEMA CUBY beds per manufacturer instructions on top of the mesh sheets at the positions marked from [bed-layout.md](bed-layout.md). Place Bed A in the east position (near trampoline) and Bed B in the west position (near stairway). Once each bed is placed, fold the excess mesh up against the inner walls.

**Verify:** Both beds are level, positioned per layout, mesh folded up inside walls with no gaps, all sides accessible with paths between them.

### Step 3: Install Reservoir in Bed B South Zone (~30 min)

- **Time:** ~30 min
- **People:** 1 adult (child should step back -- sharp tools and heavy liner)

Follow [reservoir-build.md](reservoir-build.md) for the Bed B south zone only. This includes lining the south zone of the bed, perforating and laying PVC pipes, installing the fill tube and overflow fitting, and filling the reservoir zone with gravel and geotextile.

The south zone of Bed B holds the warm crops (cucumber, tomato, pepper, basil) which have high water demand -- the reservoir is essential here. The north zone (raspberries + chives) does not need a reservoir; raspberries prefer free-draining soil.

**CRITICAL:** Test the reservoir with water before proceeding. Fill via the fill tube until overflow triggers. Fix any issues now -- they are impossible to fix after soil is added.

**Verify:** Water fills via tube, exits via overflow, no leaks visible. See the 4-step testing procedure in [reservoir-build.md](reservoir-build.md).

### Step 4: Apply Copper Tape to Beds A and B (~20 min)

- **Time:** ~10 min per bed
- **People:** 1 adult
- **Child can:** Hold the tape roll, press tape firmly against the bed

Follow [slug-defense.md](slug-defense.md) for the application method. Clean the steel surface with rubbing alcohol, apply the tape in a continuous band ~5cm below the top rim, fold at corners (do not cut), and overlap the ends by at least 2cm.

**Important:** Apply IMMEDIATELY on new steel. The adhesive bonds much better before the rust patina develops. Do not wait.

**Verify:** Continuous band around each bed, no gaps at corners, overlapped at joints.

### Step 5: Assemble Bed C (150x40 on Wheels) on Cobblestones (~15 min)

- **Time:** ~15 min
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Help wheel the bed into position

Position Bed C on the cobblestones near the north fence. This bed comes pre-assembled with wheels from byJEMA -- it may just need placement and locking of the wheels.

**Note:** No mole mesh needed -- Bed C is on cobblestones, not soil.

**Verify:** Bed is level, wheels locked, positioned per layout.

### Step 6: Apply Copper Tape to Bed C (~10 min)

- **Time:** ~10 min
- **People:** 1 adult
- **Child can:** Same as Step 4

Follow [slug-defense.md](slug-defense.md). Same method as the other beds.

**Verify:** Continuous band around the bed, no gaps at corners, overlapped at joints.

## Day 2: Soil Fill + Trellis (~2.5-3 hours)

### Step 7: Fill Bed B South Zone (Reservoir Bed) (~25 min)

- **Time:** ~25 min
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Spread the top hojbedsmuld layer (fun, dirty, and manageable)

The reservoir is already installed from Day 1. Add the garden waste layer on top of the geotextile, then hojbedsmuld. See [soil-layers.md](soil-layers.md), noting the reservoir modification -- the drainage gravel layer is replaced by the reservoir system.

Layer sequence above the reservoir:
1. Garden waste (branches, leaves, clippings): 10-15cm
2. Hojbedsmuld (raised bed soil mix): 15-20cm

**Verify:** Layers at correct depths, fill tube accessible above soil line.

### Step 8: Fill Bed B North Zone (Standard 3-Layer) (~20 min)

- **Time:** ~20 min
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Help spread all three layers

Follow [soil-layers.md](soil-layers.md) for the standard bed fill:
1. Drainage gravel (16-32mm): 8-10cm
2. Garden waste (branches, leaves, clippings): 10-15cm
3. Hojbedsmuld (raised bed soil mix): 15-20cm

**Verify:** Drainage layer 8-10cm, waste layer 10-15cm, soil layer 15-20cm.

### Step 9: Fill Bed A (Standard 3-Layer) (~20 min)

- **Time:** ~20 min
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Help spread all three layers

Follow [soil-layers.md](soil-layers.md) for the standard 40cm bed:
1. Drainage gravel (16-32mm): 8-10cm
2. Garden waste (branches, leaves, clippings): 10-15cm
3. Hojbedsmuld (raised bed soil mix): 15-20cm

**Verify:** Drainage layer 8-10cm, waste layer 10-15cm, soil layer 15-20cm.

### Step 10: Fill Bed C (Standard 3-Layer) (~15 min)

- **Time:** ~15 min
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Help spread all three layers

Follow [soil-layers.md](soil-layers.md) for the standard bed fill. No LECA or lightweight materials needed -- Bed C is on wheels on cobblestones, not on a weight-sensitive terrace.

1. Drainage gravel (16-32mm): 5-8cm
2. Garden waste (branches, leaves, clippings): 10-12cm
3. Hojbedsmuld (raised bed soil mix): 15-18cm

**Verify:** Layers at correct depths, total fill height ~30-38cm (allowing for settling).

### Step 11: Build and Install A-Frame Trellis on Bed B South End (~60 min)

- **Time:** ~60 min
- **People:** 1-2 adults
- **Child can:** Hold mesh panels during attachment, test stability after

Follow [trellis-build.md](trellis-build.md) completely. Build the two mesh panels, connect them at the ridge with hinges, spread the legs to 70cm base width, and position the A-frame over the south end of Bed B (where the cucumbers will go) with legs on either side of the bed.

Anchor the legs to prevent wind toppling -- use ground stakes, L-brackets, or weighted bases.

**Verify:** Stable under firm push from each side, mesh securely attached, peak at ~120cm.

### Step 12: First Watering (~15 min)

- **Time:** ~15 min
- **People:** 1 adult or child with hose
- **Child can:** Do most of this step independently with a hose or watering can

Water all 3 beds thoroughly from the top:
1. Water each bed until water drains from the bottom
2. Fill the reservoir in Bed B south zone via the fill tube until overflow triggers
3. Let water soak in for 5-10 minutes, then water again

Beds will settle 5-10cm over the next week. Plan to top up with hojbedsmuld before planting (Phase 2).

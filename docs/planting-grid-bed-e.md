**Orientation:** Long axis roughly N-S along railing, path side = interior (house side)

### Detailed Layout Description

**Borage (center-south, from Phase 2):**
- 1 borage plant at (15, 30) -- established from Phase 2
- 30-40cm spread, pollinator attractor
- Good companion for broccoli and beans (attracts beneficial insects)

**Calendula (edges, from Phase 2):**
- Calendula #1 at southwest corner (5, 10) -- pest deterrent
- Calendula #2 at northeast corner (35, 75) -- pest deterrent
- Stays through Phase 3 -- attracts beneficial insects, deters pests near broccoli

**Phase 3: Broccoli (center-north):**
- 1 Calabrese broccoli transplant at (20, 55)
- Needs 30-45cm clearance from neighbors -- positioned with space from borage and bean row
- Transplant seedling W21 (May 20+)
- Harvest main head, then side shoots continue for weeks of extended harvest
- Good companion with beans (nitrogen fixers) and dill (hoverfly attraction)

**Phase 3: Bush bean row (east edge):**
- 6 bush beans (Provider or Speedy) in a column along the east edge at W=35cm
- 12cm apart: positions at L=10, 22, 34, 46, 58, 70
- Direct-sow W20 (May 15) -- 3-5cm deep, one seed per hole
- 50-55 days to harvest (early-mid July)
- Nitrogen fixers -- good for soil and companion to broccoli
- Bush variety, no staking needed (30-45cm tall)

**Phase 3: Dill (southwest corner):**
- 1 dill plant at (10, 70)
- Direct-sow W18 (May 1) -- 0.5cm deep
- Attracts hoverflies that eat broccoli aphids and cabbage pests
- Good companion for broccoli
- Self-sows if allowed to flower

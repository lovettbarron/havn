# Shopping List: Havn Garden Infrastructure

**Project:** Havn -- A Learning Garden
**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Location:** Vejle, Denmark

This shopping list covers all materials needed to build, fill, and equip 5 raised beds (3 backyard + 2 terrace) with slug defense, self-watering reservoirs, and a cucumber trellis.

**Two pricing options are provided:**
- **Option A (Recommended):** Hybrid approach -- corten steel for the 3 visible backyard beds, galvanized steel for the 2 terrace beds. Premium appearance, 20+ year lifespan, zero maintenance.
- **Option B (Budget):** All galvanized steel. Same durability and function, less visual warmth. Saves 600-1,800 DKK on the beds alone.

All prices are in DKK and were sourced in March 2026. Prices marked with ~ are estimates that should be confirmed with the supplier before ordering.

## Potential Savings

- **Garden waste fill is free:** Branches, leaves, and grass clippings from your own garden fill the middle layer of each bed, saving 30-40% vs purchasing all soil.
- **Gravel sourced locally:** Drainage gravel from a local byggemarked is cheaper than online delivery. Ask about bulk/leftover prices.
- **The beds are the biggest cost driver:** 65-70% of the total budget goes to the 5 metal beds. If budget is tight, consider buying 3 beds first (backyard) and adding terrace beds later.
- **Trellis from scrap wood:** If you have leftover treated timber or access to pallet wood, trellis cost drops to near zero.
- **Compare soil suppliers:** Get delivery quotes from both sandshoppen.dk and droemmehave.dk to your address in Vejle. Delivery surcharges vary significantly.

## Notes and Ordering Tips

### byJEMA Beds (Lines 1-2)
- **Lead time:** 1-3 day handling + 2-7 day transit. Order as early as possible to avoid delays.
- **Assembly:** Self-assembly kits. No tools required beyond what ships in the box.
- **Corten patina:** Corten steel arrives raw/silver and develops its characteristic rust-orange patina over weeks to months of outdoor exposure. This is intentional and protective -- not a defect.

### Terrace Bed Dimensions
- **Action required:** Contact byJEMA to confirm availability of 80x40x**30**cm galvanized beds. The standard CUBY line uses 40cm height.
- **Fallback:** If 30cm height is not available, 80x40x**40**cm works. The extra 10cm adds approximately 20 kg of saturated soil weight per bed. See `docs/bed-layout.md` for weight calculations.

### Soil Delivery
- **Action required:** Get delivery quotes to your address in Vejle from:
  - [sandshoppen.dk](https://www.sandshoppen.dk/hoejbedsmuld/) -- ~1,500 DKK/m3 (price confirmed for Herning delivery; Vejle may differ)
  - [droemmehave.dk](https://www.droemmehave.dk) -- compare pricing and delivery terms
- **Volume needed:** ~0.6 m3 total for the top growing layer across all 5 beds. You may want to order 0.7 m3 to have a small reserve.

### Copper Tape (Line 6)
- **Timing:** Apply copper tape on bed assembly day, before the corten patina develops. New steel provides better adhesive grip.
- **Application:** Clean the surface with rubbing alcohol first. Apply in a continuous band around each bed exterior, ~5cm below the top rim. Overlap ends by at least 2cm. Fold at corners rather than cutting.

### Byggemarked Items (Lines 4, 7-19)
- **One trip:** Most reservoir, trellis, and drainage items can be bought in a single byggemarked visit (Silvan or Bauhaus in Vejle).
- **Bring the list:** Print this shopping list or save it to your phone. The in-store staff can help locate PVC fittings and geotextile.

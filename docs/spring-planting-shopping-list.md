# Spring Planting Shopping List -- Phase 2 Plants

**Phase:** 02-spring-planting
**Purpose:** All plants, seeds, and craft materials needed for Phase 2 spring planting (W16-W18)
**Purchase timing:** W14-W16 (see Shopping Timeline below)

> **Bare-root raspberry canes have the longest lead time.** Order online by W14 if your local garden center does not stock Autumn Bliss. Strawberry plugs and herb plants should be bought as fresh as possible -- ideally the day before or day of planting.

## Practical Notes

- **Danish garden centers** (Plantorama, Bilka Garden, Harald Nyborg) stock most herb plants, strawberry plugs, and seed packets in April. Go early in the season for the best selection.
- **Autumn Bliss raspberry canes:** This is a specific primocane variety (fruits on first-year canes). If your local garden center only has floricane varieties (e.g., Glen Ample), those fruit on second-year canes -- still fine, but harvest is delayed to Year 2. For first-year fruit, insist on Autumn Bliss or another primocane type. Online: plantetorvet.dk, blomsterlandet.dk, or groenthansen.dk.
- **Strawberry plugs** are often sold in trays of 3, 6, or 10. A tray of 3 Ostara and buying 1 Korona separately is ideal. If Korona is unavailable, any June-bearing variety (e.g., Elsanta, Honeoye) is a fine substitute.
- **Creeping thyme:** Often labeled "Thymus serpyllum" or "krybende timian" in Danish garden centers. Buy small pots -- they spread quickly.
- **No overlap with warm-season list:** All seeds and plants on this list are distinct from those on the warm-season shopping list (`docs/warm-season-shopping-list.md`). No double-buying needed.
